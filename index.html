History Learning Platform

A teacher guided platform for Government, World Geography, and history topics, built for student progress tracking, discussion work, file uploads, and AI supported learning.

What this platform will do

Student Learning Paths

Students move through lessons, videos, short responses, quizzes, and projects in one guided sequence.

Teacher Dashboard

Track progress, review submissions, grade work, leave feedback, and manage weighted categories.

Built In + Upload Assignments

Support discussions, short responses, quizzes, and uploads like PDF, Word, PowerPoint, Pages, and Keynote.

AI Support

Give students help with explanations, review questions, revision support, and guided thinking inside lessons.
