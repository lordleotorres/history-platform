Lesson 5 Activity: Cold War in Asia

Examine how different leaders understood communism and conflict. Compare their language, fears, and goals.

Tuyên Ngôn Độc Lập
Declaration of Independence of Vietnam

Ho Chi Minh • September 2, 1945

“All men are created equal… all peoples have a right to live, to be happy and free.”

“The French imperialists… have deprived our people of every democratic liberty… they have built more prisons than schools… they have slain our patriots.”

Statement on Korea

Harry S. Truman • June 27, 1950

“Communism has passed beyond the use of subversion… and will now use armed invasion and war.”

“I have ordered United States air and sea forces to give the Korean Government troops cover and support.”

National Emergency Proclamation

Harry S. Truman • December 16, 1950

“World conquest by communist imperialism is the goal of the forces of aggression…”

“The people… would no longer enjoy freedom of speech… freedom of worship… or the right to choose their government.”
