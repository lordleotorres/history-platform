Key Vocabulary

  • Capitalism – private businesses and free markets
  • Communism – government controls the economy
  • Iron Curtain – division between Eastern and Western Europe
  • Containment – stopping the spread of communism

The 3 Causes of the Cold War

1. Different Ideologies
The U.S. believed in freedom, elections, and capitalism. The Soviet Union believed in communism and control.

2. End of World War II
Both countries became superpowers after WWII. They competed to influence Europe.

3. Fear and Mistrust
Each side believed the other was dangerous. This fear led to tension and conflict.
