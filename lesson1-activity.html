Compare Two Perspectives

How did the U.S. and Soviet Union view the world differently?

Goal:

Compare a U.S. and Soviet source to understand why conflict grew after WWII.

Source A — United States

Harry Truman, 1947

"Free peoples must be supported against groups that seek to control them through force or pressure."

Source B — Soviet Union

Soviet Perspective

"Capitalist nations seek to dominate weaker countries. Socialist nations must resist imperialism."

Your Analysis

1. What does the United States believe is its role?
2. What does the Soviet Union believe is happening?
3. Why would these two views create conflict?
